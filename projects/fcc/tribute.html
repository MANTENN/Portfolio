Elon Musk

The man behind Tesla, SpaceX and MarsOne.

Early Life

1971 - born on June 28, in Pretoria, Guateng, South Africa to Maye Musk and Errol Musk.

1981 - became interested in computing with the Commodor VIC-20.

1983 - taught himself computer programming to develop a video game he called Blastar, which he sold to a magainze called PC and Office Technology, for approximately 500 dollars.

1988 - accepted into Queens University in Toronto, Canada.

1990 - transfered to the university of Pennsylvania.

Career

1995 - launched a startup with his brother Kimbal, called Zip2, only with 2,000 dollars from their father. The company marketed and developed a internet guide for the newspaper publishing business.

1999 - Compaq acquired Zip2 for 307 million dollars in cash and 32 million dolalrs in stock, of which he recieved 22 million dollars.

1999 - March, Elon started-up X.com.

2000 - ousted from his role as CEO, remained on board.

2001 - renamed X.com and Confinity service to PayPal.

2001 - Conceptualized "Mars Oasis"; a project to terraform Mars into Earth like environment.

2001 - October Elon traveled to Moscow with Jim Cantrell and Adeo Ressi, to purchase a refurbished Dnepr Intercontinental ballistic missiles (ICBM); returned empty handed home.

2002 - Ebay acquired PayPal for 1.5 billion dollars in stock, of which he recieved 165 million dollars.

2002 - February, the group returned to Moscow along with Mike Griffin; walked out empty handed again because he tought it was to expesnive. On his flight back home, Musk realized he could start a company that could build affordable rockets he needed

2002 - May, founded Space Exploration Technologies(SpaceX) with 100 million dollars of his fortune.

2004 - led the Series A round of investment - took an active role within the company and oversaw product design of the Tesla Roadster.

2006 - co-founded with his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive - provided the initial concept and financial capital

2008 following the finanicial crisis, Elon assumed leadership of the company as CEO and product architect

2012 - announced the collaberation of SolarCity with Tesla to reduce the effect of greenhouse gasses.

2013 - August 12, unveiled a concept for a high-speed transportation system - utilized reduced pressure tubes in which pressurized capsules ride on air cussion powered by linear induction motors and air compressors.

2014 - announced Tesla would allow its technology patents to be used by anyone in good faith to entice automobile manufacturers to speed up development of electric cars.

2014 - June 17, built a Solar City production facility, triple the size of the largest solar plant in the US.

2015 - June, announced a design competition for students and others to build Hyperloop pods

2015 - December, announced the creation of OpenAI - non-profit artificial intelligence research company, created "to countereact large corporations who may gain too much pwoer by owning super-intelligence systems devoted to profits, as well as governments which may use AI to gain power and even opress their citizenry"(Elon Musk).

2016 - Feburary, acquired the domain: Tesla.com

2016 - Tesla submitted an offer to acquire SolarCity

2016 - December 17, stuck in traffic, tweeted he will build a tunnel boring machine - created The Boring Company

2016 - co-founded Nueralink - nuerotechnology startup company seeking to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence.

2017 - January 21, tweeted "Exciting progress on the tunnel front. Plan to start digging in a month or so."

2017 - February, the company begun digging a 30 foot wide, 50 foot long, and 15 foot deep "test trench" on the premises of Space X's, since it requires no permits.

As of 2014 - annual salary is one dollar, the remainder of his compensation is in the form of stock and performance-based bonuses.
